1. THE APPLICATION

Sketcho is an augmented reality (AR) drawing application that allows users to create drawings in 3D space using their device's camera and motion sensors. The Application runs fully on-device. We do not collect or store personal data on our servers. Optional iCloud sync uses the user's Apple iCloud account to keep drawings in sync across devices. Camera access is used solely to render the AR scene and is processed on-device; no camera frames are stored or transmitted to us.

3. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

The Application requires:

  • An AR-capable device (e.g., ARKit/RealityKit compatible)

  • Motion sensors (accelerometer/gyroscope) for spatial tracking
  • Optional: iCloud enabled for cross-device sync
  • Storage space for local drawings
