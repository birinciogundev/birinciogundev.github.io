Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13.08.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Sketcho handles your information. Sketcho processes everything on-device; we do not collect personal data. Optional iCloud sync stores your content in your Apple iCloud account.

Information Collection and Use

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, contact info)
  • No analytics or tracking identifiers
  • No camera frames or sensor data sent to our servers

Information Processed On-Device

  • AR session data (camera and motion sensors) used solely to render AR; not stored by us
  • Your AR drawings and related metadata stored locally on your device

Optional iCloud Sync

  • If enabled, drawings and related metadata are stored in your iCloud account
  • Apple's privacy and security policies apply to iCloud storage
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device settings

Device Permissions

  • Camera: Required only to render AR content; frames are processed on-device and not saved by us
  • Motion Sensors: Used for spatial tracking during AR sessions
  • Photos/Files (optional): Only if you export or save captures; saved to your device or iCloud

Children

Sketcho does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. As we do not collect personal data at all, there is no data for us to delete. If you have concerns, manage app data locally on your device or via iCloud settings.

Security

  • All processing occurs on-device
  • Local data remains on your device unless you choose to sync via iCloud
  • iCloud storage is secured by Apple

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we encourage you to keep your device and Apple ID secure.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date and posting the new policy here.

Your Control

  • Delete drawings locally at any time
  • Disable iCloud sync in device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact details on the App Store listing or the developer website.
