Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09.01.2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Dreamatix collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection and Use

Information We Collect

  • Dream Content: The descriptions of dreams you input for interpretation
  • Generated Content: AI-interpreted dream analyses and generated images
  • Device Information: Operating system, device model, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app's features
  • Storage Permission: Access to device gallery (only when saving images)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your dreams through our AI interpretation system
  • To generate visual representations of your dreams
  • To improve our dream interpretation accuracy
  • To enhance the app's functionality and user experience
  • To provide technical support when needed

Third Party Access

We work with the following trusted third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Processes dream descriptions for interpretation
  • Firebase: Stores anonymous dream data and analytics
  • Google Play Services: Handles app functionality and updates

These services may collect and process your data according to their respective privacy policies.

Opt-Out Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your dream records at any time
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Disable image saving functionality
  • Request complete deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at dreamatix.prod@gmail.com

Data Retention Policy

We retain your information as follows:

  • Dream records: Until you choose to delete them
  • Generated images: Stored locally on your device only
  • Usage analytics: For up to 12 months
  • Error logs: 30 days

You can request deletion of your data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

Children

Dreamatix does not knowingly collect or solicit information from anyone under 13 years of age. If you are under 13:

  • Do not use or access the Application
  • Do not send us any personal information
  • Do not download the Application

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored anonymously
  • Secure data transmission using encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to user data by authorized personnel only
  • Secure Firebase infrastructure

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the Application after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Your Consent

By using Dreamatix, you consent to:

  • The collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy
  • The processing of your dream content through AI systems
  • The storage of anonymous data in Firebase
  • The use of device gallery access for saving images

You can withdraw your consent at any time by uninstalling the Application or contacting us.
